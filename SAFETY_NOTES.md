# Safety Notes

- This V2 repo does **not** mark any workflow clinically approved.
- This V2 repo does **not** mark any workflow clinically tested.
- The 12 limited-testing excluded workflows remain hidden and blocked.
- Outputs are clinician-review drafts only.
- No autonomous diagnosis, treatment, dose, investigation, referral, or follow-up should be invented.
- Red flags are prompts only, not conclusions.
- Patient instructions are only shown when explicitly entered by the clinician in the Detailed Encounter flow.
