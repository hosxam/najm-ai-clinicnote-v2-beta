1. `gp_explicit_mapping_ledger_0626_0675.json` is a pre-populated clinical object imported by every migrated caller.

6. The guard's static prohibition is limited to files importing `gpBatchSupport.mjs` and exact symbol names; renamed wrappers bypass it.
