<h2 class="pane-title">Clinical Practice Guideline: Tympanostomy Tubes in Children <strong>(Update)</strong></h2>
<p>The purpose of this clinical practice guideline is to reassess and update recommendations in the prior guideline from 2013 and to provide clinicians with trustworthy, evidence-based recommendations on patient selection and surgical indications for managing tympanostomy tubes in children. This guideline is intended for any clinician involved in managing children, aged 6 months to 12 years, with tympanostomy tubes or children being considered for tympanostomy tubes in any care setting as an intervention for otitis media of any type.</p>
<h2><strong>Leadership</strong></h2>
<p>Richard M. Rosenfeld, MD, MPH, MBA (<em>Chair</em>); David E. Tunkel, MD (<em>Assistant Chair</em>); Seth R. Schwartz, MD, MPH (<em>Methodologist</em>)</p>

<p>Samantha Anne, MD, MS; Charles E. Bishop, AuD, PhD, CCC-A; Daniel C. Chelius, MD; Jesse Hackell, MD; Lisa L. Hunter, PhD; Kristina L. Keppel, DNP, APNP, CPNP; Ana H. Kim, MD; Tae W. Walker, PhD, CCC-A/SLP; Taskin M. Monjur</p>
