<div class="chapter" title="Recommendations" id="ng132-recommendations" xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml">
  <h2 class="title" id="recommendations">Recommendations</h2>
  <div class="panel panel--primary">
    <p>People have the right to be involved in discussions and make informed decisions about their care, as described in <a class="link" href="https://www.nice.org.uk/about/nice-communities/nice-and-the-public/making-decisions-about-your-care" target="_top">NICE's information on making decisions about your care</a>. </p>
    <p>
      <a class="link" href="https://www.nice.org.uk/about/what-we-do/our-programmes/nice-guidance/nice-guidelines/using-NICE-guidelines-to-make-decisions" target="_top">Making decisions using NICE guidelines</a> explains how we use words to show the strength (or certainty) of our recommendations and has information about prescribing medicines (including off‑label use), professional guidelines, standards and laws (including on consent and mental capacity) and safeguarding.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="section" title="1.1 Diagnostic testing in primary care" id="ng132-diagnostic-testing-in-primary-care">
    <h3 class="title" id="diagnostic-testing-in-primary-care">1.1 Diagnostic testing in primary care</h3>
    <div class="section" title="Measuring albumin-adjusted serum calcium" id="ng132-measuring-albumin-adjusted-serum-calcium">
      <h4 class="title" id="measuring-albumin-adjusted-serum-calcium">Measuring albumin-adjusted serum calcium</h4>
      <article id="ng132-1_1_1" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.1.1</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Measure albumin-adjusted serum calcium for people with any of the following features, which might indicate primary hyperparathyroidism: </p>
          <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>symptoms of <a class="link" href="recommendations#hypercalcaemia" target="_top" rel="internal" data-chapter="recommendations" data-original-url="#hypercalcaemia">hypercalcaemia</a>, such as thirst, frequent or excessive urination, or constipation </p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>osteoporosis or a previous fragility fracture (for recommendations on assessing the risk of fragility fracture in people with osteoporosis, see the <a class="link" href="https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/cg146" target="_top" data-original-url="https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/cg146">NICE guideline on osteoporosis</a>)</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>a renal stone (for recommendations on assessing and managing renal stones, see the <a class="link" href="https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/ng118" target="_top" data-original-url="https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/ng118">NICE guideline on renal and ureteric stones</a>)</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>an incidental finding of elevated albumin-adjusted serum calcium (2.6 mmol/litre or above).</p>
            </li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng132-1_1_2" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.1.2</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Consider measuring albumin-adjusted serum calcium for people with <a class="link" href="recommendations#chronic-non-differentiated-symptoms" target="_top" rel="internal" data-chapter="recommendations" data-original-url="#chronic-non-differentiated-symptoms">chronic non-differentiated symptoms</a>.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng132-1_1_3" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.1.3</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Do not measure ionised calcium when testing for primary hyperparathyroidism.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng132-1_1_4" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.1.4</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Repeat the albumin-adjusted serum calcium measurement at least once if the first measurement is either:</p>
          <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>2.6 mmol/litre or above <strong>or</strong></p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>2.5 mmol/litre or above and features of primary hyperparathyroidism are present.<br /><br />Base the decision to carry out further repeat measurements on the level of albumin-adjusted serum calcium and the person's symptoms.</p>
            </li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </article>
    </div>
    <div class="section" title="Measuring parathyroid hormone" id="ng132-measuring-parathyroid-hormone">
      <h4 class="title" id="measuring-parathyroid-hormone">Measuring parathyroid hormone</h4>
      <article id="ng132-1_1_5" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.1.5</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Measure parathyroid hormone (PTH) for people whose albumin-adjusted serum calcium level is either:</p>
          <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>2.6 mmol/litre or above on at least 2 separate occasions <strong>or</strong></p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>2.5 mmol/litre or above on at least 2 separate occasions and primary hyperparathyroidism is suspected.</p>
            </li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng132-1_1_6" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.1.6</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>When measuring PTH, use a random sample and do a concurrent measurement of the albumin-adjusted serum calcium level. </p>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng132-1_1_7" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.1.7</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Do not routinely repeat PTH measurement in primary care.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng132-1_1_8" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.1.8</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Seek <a class="link" href="recommendations#advice-from-a-specialist" target="_top" rel="internal" data-chapter="recommendations" data-original-url="#advice-from-a-specialist">advice from a specialist</a> with expertise in primary hyperparathyroidism if the person's PTH measurement is either:</p>
          <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>above the midpoint of the reference range and primary hyperparathyroidism is suspected <strong>or</strong></p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>below the midpoint of the reference range with a concurrent albumin-adjusted serum calcium level of 2.6 mmol/litre or above.</p>
            </li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng132-1_1_9" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.1.9</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Do not offer further investigations for primary hyperparathyroidism if:</p>
          <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>the person's PTH is within the reference range but below the midpoint of the reference range <strong>and</strong></p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>their concurrent albumin-adjusted serum calcium level is below 2.6 mmol/litre.</p>
            </li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng132-1_1_10" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.1.10</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Look for alternative diagnoses, including malignancy, if the person's PTH is below the lower limit of the reference range.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
      <div class="panel">
        <p>For a short explanation of why the committee made the recommendations and how they might affect practice, see the <a class="link" href="rationale-and-impact#diagnostic-testing-in-primary-care-2" target="_top" rel="internal" data-chapter="rationale-and-impact" data-original-url="#diagnostic-testing-in-primary-care-2">rationale and impact section on diagnostic testing in primary care</a>.</p>
        <p>Full details of the evidence and the committee's discussion are in <a class="link" href="https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/ng132/evidence/a-indications-for-diagnostic-testing-pdf-6782710574" target="_top">evidence review A: indications for diagnostic testing</a> and <a class="link" href="https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/ng132/evidence/b-diagnostic-tests-pdf-6782710575" target="_top">evidence review B: diagnostic tests</a>.</p>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="section" title="1.2 Testing and assessment in secondary care" id="ng132-testing-and-assessment-in-secondary-care">
    <h3 class="title" id="testing-and-assessment-in-secondary-care">1.2 Testing and assessment in secondary care</h3>
    <div class="section" title="Measuring vitamin D" id="ng132-measuring-vitamin-d">
      <h4 class="title" id="measuring-vitamin-d">Measuring vitamin D</h4>
      <article id="ng132-1_2_1" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.2.1</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>For people with a probable diagnosis of primary hyperparathyroidism, measure vitamin D and offer vitamin D supplements if needed. </p>
        </div>
      </article>
    </div>
    <div class="section" title="Excluding familial hypocalciuric hypercalcaemia" id="ng132-excluding-familial-hypocalciuric-hypercalcaemia">
      <h4 class="title" id="excluding-familial-hypocalciuric-hypercalcaemia">Excluding familial hypocalciuric hypercalcaemia</h4>
      <article id="ng132-1_2_2" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.2.2</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>To differentiate primary hyperparathyroidism from familial hypocalciuric hypercalcaemia, measure urine calcium excretion using any one of the following tests:</p>
          <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>24-hour urinary calcium excretion</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>random renal calcium:creatinine excretion ratio</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>random calcium:creatinine clearance ratio.</p>
            </li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </article>
    </div>
    <div class="section" title="Assessment after diagnosis" id="ng132-assessment-after-diagnosis">
      <h4 class="title" id="assessment-after-diagnosis">Assessment after diagnosis</h4>
      <article id="ng132-1_2_3" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.2.3</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>For people with a confirmed diagnosis of primary hyperparathyroidism: </p>
          <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>assess symptoms and comorbidities </p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>measure eGFR (estimated glomerular filtration rate) or serum creatinine </p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>do a DXA (dual-energy X‑ray absorptiometry) scan of the lumbar spine, distal radius and hip</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>do an ultrasound scan of the renal tract. </p>
            </li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </article>
      <div class="panel">
        <p>For a short explanation of why the committee made the recommendations and how they might affect practice, see the <a class="link" href="rationale-and-impact#testing-and-assessment-in-secondary-care-2" target="_top" rel="internal" data-chapter="rationale-and-impact" data-original-url="#testing-and-assessment-in-secondary-care-2">rationale and impact section on testing and assessment in secondary care</a>.</p>
        <p>Full details of the evidence and the committee's discussion are in <a class="link" href="https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/ng132/evidence/a-indications-for-diagnostic-testing-pdf-6782710574" target="_top">evidence review A: indications for diagnostic testing</a> and <a class="link" href="https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/ng132/evidence/b-diagnostic-tests-pdf-6782710575" target="_top">evidence review B: diagnostic tests</a>.</p>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="section" title="1.3 Referral for surgery" id="ng132-referral-for-surgery">
    <h3 class="title" id="referral-for-surgery">1.3 Referral for surgery</h3>
    <article id="ng132-1_3_1" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
      <h4 class="recommendation__number">1.3.1</h4>
      <div class="recommendation__body">
        <p>Refer people with a confirmed diagnosis of primary hyperparathyroidism to a surgeon with expertise in parathyroid surgery if they have: </p>
        <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
          <li class="listitem">
            <p>symptoms of hypercalcaemia such as thirst, frequent or excessive urination, or constipation <strong>or</strong></p>
          </li>
          <li class="listitem">
            <p>end-organ disease (renal stones, fragility fractures or osteoporosis) <strong>or</strong></p>
          </li>
          <li class="listitem">
            <p>an albumin-adjusted serum calcium level of 2.85 mmol/litre or above.</p>
          </li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </article>
    <article id="ng132-1_3_2" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
      <h4 class="recommendation__number">1.3.2</h4>
      <div class="recommendation__body">
        <p>Consider referral to a surgeon with expertise in parathyroid surgery for people with a confirmed diagnosis of primary hyperparathyroidism even if they do not have the features listed in recommendation 1.3.1.</p>
      </div>
    </article>
    <div class="panel">
      <p>For a short explanation of why the committee made the recommendations and how they might affect practice, see the <a class="link" href="rationale-and-impact#referral-for-surgery-2" target="_top" rel="internal" data-chapter="rationale-and-impact" data-original-url="#referral-for-surgery-2">rationale and impact section on referral for surgery</a>.</p>
      <p>Full details of the evidence and the committee's discussion are in <a class="link" href="https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/ng132/evidence/c-indications-for-surgery-pdf-6782710576" target="_top">evidence review C: indications for surgery</a>. </p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="section" title="1.4 Surgical management" id="ng132-surgical-management">
    <h3 class="title" id="surgical-management">1.4 Surgical management</h3>
    <div class="section" title="Preoperative imaging" id="ng132-preoperative-imaging">
      <h4 class="title" id="preoperative-imaging">Preoperative imaging</h4>
      <article id="ng132-1_4_1" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.4.1</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Offer preoperative imaging (usually ultrasound) to people having surgery for primary hyperparathyroidism if it will inform the surgical approach. </p>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng132-1_4_2" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.4.2</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Consider a second preoperative imaging modality (usually a sestamibi scan) if it will further guide the surgical approach.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng132-1_4_3" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.4.3</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Do not offer more preoperative imaging if the first-modality (usually ultrasound) and second-modality scans (usually a sestamibi scan) do not identify an adenoma or are discordant. </p>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng132-1_4_4" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.4.4</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Proceed with surgery, performed by a surgeon with expertise in 4‑gland exploration, even if preoperative imaging has not identified an adenoma.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng132-1_4_5" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.4.5</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>If preoperative imaging shows an ectopic adenoma, refer the person to a centre with the relevant expertise.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
    </div>
    <div class="section" title="Type of surgery" id="ng132-type-of-surgery">
      <h4 class="title" id="type-of-surgery">Type of surgery</h4>
      <article id="ng132-1_4_6" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.4.6</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Offer a choice of either 4‑gland exploration or focused parathyroidectomy to people whose preoperative imaging shows a single adenoma in the neck. Discuss the choice of surgery with the person, and explain:</p>
          <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>what happens during each type of surgery</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>how well each type of surgery works</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>what types of anaesthesia are used</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>how long each type of surgery is likely to take</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>how large the resulting scars are likely to be</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>the risks of each type of surgery.</p>
            </li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng132-1_4_7" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.4.7</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Offer 4‑gland exploration to people who have had preoperative imaging that does not identify a single adenoma.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng132-1_4_8" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.4.8</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Consider 4‑gland exploration for people having surgery for primary hyperparathyroidism whose first-modality and second-modality scans are discordant.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
    </div>
    <div class="section" title="Intraoperative PTH monitoring" id="ng132-intraoperative-pth-monitoring">
      <h4 class="title" id="intraoperative-pth-monitoring">Intraoperative PTH monitoring</h4>
      <article id="ng132-1_4_9" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.4.9</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Do not use intraoperative PTH monitoring in first-time parathyroid surgery.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
    </div>
    <div class="section" title="Follow-up after surgery" id="ng132-follow-up-after-surgery">
      <h4 class="title" id="follow-up-after-surgery">Follow-up after surgery</h4>
      <article id="ng132-1_4_10" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.4.10</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Measure albumin-adjusted serum calcium and PTH before discharge after surgery for primary hyperparathyroidism to provide baseline information for later follow‑up.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng132-1_4_11" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.4.11</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Measure albumin-adjusted serum calcium 3 to 6 months after surgery for primary hyperparathyroidism to confirm whether surgery has been successful. </p>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng132-1_4_12" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.4.12</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>If albumin-adjusted serum calcium is within the reference range 3 to 6 months after surgery for primary hyperparathyroidism, regard the surgery as successful. Monitor albumin-adjusted serum calcium once a year. See the <a class="link" href="recommendations#monitoring" target="_top" rel="internal" data-chapter="recommendations" data-original-url="#monitoring">section on monitoring</a>.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
      <div class="section" title="Unsuccessful surgery" id="ng132-unsuccessful-surgery">
        <h5 class="title" id="unsuccessful-surgery">Unsuccessful surgery</h5>
        <article id="ng132-1_4_13" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
          <h6 class="recommendation__number">1.4.13</h6>
          <div class="recommendation__body">
            <p>For people who have had unsuccessful surgery for primary hyperparathyroidism:</p>
            <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
              <li class="listitem">
                <p>conduct a multidisciplinary team (MDT) review at a specialist centre that includes:</p>
                <p>
                  <div class="itemizedlist">
                    <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
                      <li class="listitem">
                        <p>initial findings from surgery</p>
                      </li>
                      <li class="listitem">
                        <p>previous imaging and histology </p>
                      </li>
                      <li class="listitem">
                        <p>the clinical and biochemical indications for repeat surgery</p>
                      </li>
                    </ul>
                  </div>
                </p>
              </li>
              <li class="listitem">
                <p>offer monitoring as set out in the <a class="link" href="recommendations#monitoring" target="_top" rel="internal" data-chapter="recommendations" data-original-url="#monitoring">section on monitoring</a>.</p>
              </li>
            </ul>
          </div>
        </article>
        <article id="ng132-1_4_14" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
          <h6 class="recommendation__number">1.4.14</h6>
          <div class="recommendation__body">
            <p>If repeat surgery is performed for primary hyperparathyroidism, it should be done at a centre with expertise in reoperative parathyroid surgery. </p>
          </div>
        </article>
        <div class="panel">
          <p>For a short explanation of why the committee made the recommendations and how they might affect practice, see the <a class="link" href="rationale-and-impact#surgical-management-2" target="_top" rel="internal" data-chapter="rationale-and-impact" data-original-url="#surgical-management-2">rationale and impact section on surgical management</a>.</p>
          <div class="itemizedlist">
            <ul class="itemizedlist">
              <li class="listitem">
                <p>Full details of the evidence and the committee's discussion are in <a class="link" href="https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/ng132/evidence/d-surgical-localisation-pdf-6782710577" target="_top">evidence review D: surgical localisation</a> (for the recommendations on preoperative imaging)</p>
              </li>
              <li class="listitem">
                <p>
                  <a class="link" href="https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/ng132/evidence/e-surgical-interventions-pdf-6782710578" target="_top">evidence review E: surgical interventions</a> (for the recommendations on type of surgery)</p>
              </li>
              <li class="listitem">
                <p>
                  <a class="link" href="https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/ng132/evidence/f-management-options-in-failed-primary-surgery-pdf-6782710579" target="_top">evidence review F: management options in failed primary surgery</a> (for the recommendations on unsuccessful surgery)</p>
              </li>
              <li class="listitem">
                <p>
                  <a class="link" href="https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/ng132/evidence/i-monitoring-pdf-244177580628" target="_top">evidence review I: monitoring</a> (for the recommendations on follow‑up after surgery).</p>
              </li>
            </ul>
          </div>
        </div>

  <div class="section" title="1.5 Non-surgical management" id="ng132-non-surgical-management">
    <h3 class="title" id="non-surgical-management">1.5 Non-surgical management</h3>
    <div class="section" title="Calcimimetics" id="ng132-calcimimetics">
      <h4 class="title" id="calcimimetics">Calcimimetics</h4>
      <div class="panel panel--primary">
        <p>In May 2019, the use of cinacalcet after unsuccessful surgery for primary hyperparathyroidism was off label. See <a class="link" href="https://www.nice.org.uk/about/what-we-do/our-programmes/nice-guidance/nice-guidelines/making-decisions-using-nice-guidelines#prescribing-medicines" target="_top">NICE's information on prescribing medicines</a>.</p>
      </div>
      <article id="ng132-1_5_1" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.5.1</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Consider cinacalcet for people with primary hyperparathyroidism if surgery has been unsuccessful, is unsuitable or has been declined, and if their albumin-adjusted serum calcium level is either: </p>
          <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>2.85 mmol/litre or above with symptoms of hypercalcaemia <strong>or</strong></p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>3.0 mmol/litre or above with or without symptoms of hypercalcaemia.</p>
            </li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng132-1_5_2" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.5.2</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>For people whose initial albumin-adjusted serum calcium level is 2.85 mmol/litre or above with symptoms of hypercalcaemia, base decisions on whether to continue treatment with cinacalcet on how well it reduces symptoms.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng132-1_5_3" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.5.3</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>For people whose initial albumin-adjusted serum calcium level is 3.0 mmol/litre or above, base decisions on whether to continue treatment with cinacalcet on how well it reduces either symptoms or albumin-adjusted serum calcium level. </p>
        </div>
      </article>
    </div>
    <div class="section" title="Bisphosphonates" id="ng132-bisphosphonates">
      <h4 class="title" id="bisphosphonates">Bisphosphonates</h4>
      <article id="ng132-1_5_4" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.5.4</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Consider a bisphosphonate to reduce fracture risk for people with primary hyperparathyroidism and increased fracture risk.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng132-1_5_5" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.5.5</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Do not offer bisphosphonates for chronic hypercalcaemia of primary hyperparathyroidism. </p>
        </div>
      </article>
      <div class="panel">
        <p>For a short explanation of why the committee made the recommendations and how they might affect practice, see the <a class="link" href="rationale-and-impact#non-surgical-management-2" target="_top" rel="internal" data-chapter="rationale-and-impact" data-original-url="#non-surgical-management-2">rationale and impact section on non-surgical management</a>.</p>

                <a class="link" href="https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/ng132/evidence/f-management-options-in-failed-primary-surgery-pdf-6782710579" target="_top">evidence review F: management options in failed primary surgery</a>
              </p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">

                <a class="link" href="https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/ng132/evidence/g-calcimimetics-pdf-6782710580" target="_top">evidence review G: calcimimetics</a>
              </p>

  <div class="section" title="1.7 Pregnancy" id="ng132-pregnancy">
    <h3 class="title" id="pregnancy">1.7 Pregnancy</h3>
    <div class="section" title="Care before pregnancy" id="ng132-care-before-pregnancy">
      <h4 class="title" id="care-before-pregnancy">Care before pregnancy</h4>
      <article id="ng132-1_7_1" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.7.1</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Offer parathyroid surgery to women who have primary hyperparathyroidism and are considering pregnancy. </p>
        </div>
      </article>
    </div>
    <div class="section" title="Care during pregnancy" id="ng132-care-during-pregnancy">
      <h4 class="title" id="care-during-pregnancy">Care during pregnancy</h4>
      <article id="ng132-1_7_2" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.7.2</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Discuss the management of primary hyperparathyroidism for pregnant women with a MDT in a specialist centre, and refer the woman for specialist care if needed. The MDT should include: </p>
          <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>an obstetrician</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>a physician with expertise in primary hyperparathyroidism</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>a surgeon</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>a midwife</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>an anaesthetist.</p>
            </li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng132-1_7_3" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.7.3</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Do not offer cinacalcet to pregnant women with primary hyperparathyroidism. </p>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng132-1_7_4" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.7.4</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Do not offer a bisphosphonate to pregnant women with primary hyperparathyroidism.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng132-1_7_5" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.7.5</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Be aware that women with primary hyperparathyroidism are at increased risk of hypertensive disease in pregnancy. For recommendations on diagnosing and managing hypertension in pregnant women, see the <a class="link" href="https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/ng133" target="_top" data-original-url="https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/ng133">NICE guideline on hypertension in pregnancy</a>. </p>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng132-1_7_6" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.7.6</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Consult a specialist centre MDT for advice on monitoring for pregnant women with primary hyperparathyroidism.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
      <div class="panel">
        <p>For a short explanation of why the committee made the recommendations and how they might affect practice, see the <a class="link" href="rationale-and-impact#pregnancy-2" target="_top" rel="internal" data-chapter="rationale-and-impact" data-original-url="#pregnancy-2">rationale and impact section on pregnancy</a>.</p>
        <p>Full details of the evidence and the committee's discussion are in <a class="link" href="https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/ng132/evidence/j-pregnancy-pdf-244177580629" target="_top">evidence review J: pregnancy</a>.</p>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="section" title="1.8 Information and support" id="ng132-information-and-support">
    <h3 class="title" id="information-and-support">1.8 Information and support</h3>
    <article id="ng132-1_8_1" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
      <h4 class="recommendation__number">1.8.1</h4>
      <div class="recommendation__body">
        <p>Follow the recommendations on enabling people to actively participate in their care in the <a class="link" href="https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/cg138" target="_top" data-original-url="https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/cg138">NICE guideline on patient experience in adult NHS services</a>.</p>
      </div>
    </article>
    <article id="ng132-1_8_2" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
      <h4 class="recommendation__number">1.8.2</h4>
      <div class="recommendation__body">
        <p>Give people with primary hyperparathyroidism information about the condition, including:</p>
        <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
          <li class="listitem">
            <p>what primary hyperparathyroidism is</p>
          </li>
          <li class="listitem">
            <p>what the parathyroid glands do</p>
          </li>
          <li class="listitem">
            <p>causes of primary hyperparathyroidism</p>
          </li>
          <li class="listitem">
            <p>symptoms </p>
          </li>
          <li class="listitem">
            <p>diagnosis, including diagnosis if calcium or PTH levels are normal</p>
          </li>
          <li class="listitem">
            <p>prognosis </p>
          </li>
          <li class="listitem">
            <p>possible effects on daily life </p>
          </li>
          <li class="listitem">
            <p>possible long-term effects.</p>
          </li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </article>
    <article id="ng132-1_8_3" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
      <h4 class="recommendation__number">1.8.3</h4>
      <div class="recommendation__body">
        <p>Give people information about treatments for primary hyperparathyroidism that includes:</p>
        <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
          <li class="listitem">
            <p>the surgical and non-surgical treatments that are available </p>
          </li>
          <li class="listitem">
            <p>how well the treatments are likely to work</p>
          </li>
          <li class="listitem">
            <p>the advantages and disadvantages of each treatment, including possible complications and side effects </p>
          </li>
          <li class="listitem">
            <p>why these particular treatments are being offered </p>
          </li>
          <li class="listitem">
            <p>why other treatments are not advised.</p>
          </li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </article>
    <article id="ng132-1_8_4" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
      <h4 class="recommendation__number">1.8.4</h4>
      <div class="recommendation__body">
        <p>Give advice on how to reduce the symptoms of primary hyperparathyroidism and prepare for surgery or other treatment, including:</p>
        <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
          <li class="listitem">
            <p>exercise</p>
          </li>
          <li class="listitem">
            <p>diet</p>
          </li>
          <li class="listitem">
            <p>hydration</p>
          </li>
          <li class="listitem">
            <p>pain relief</p>
          </li>
          <li class="listitem">
            <p>what to expect after treatment, recovery time and return to daily activities, including return to work.</p>
          </li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </article>
    <article id="ng132-1_8_5" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
      <h4 class="recommendation__number">1.8.5</h4>
      <div class="recommendation__body">
        <p>Discuss ongoing care and monitoring for primary hyperparathyroidism, explaining the type and frequency of monitoring that will be offered and the purpose of each. See the <a class="link" href="recommendations#monitoring" target="_top" rel="internal" data-chapter="recommendations" data-original-url="#monitoring">section on monitoring</a>.</p>
      </div>
    </article>
    <div class="panel">
      <p>For a short explanation of why the committee made the recommendations and how they might affect practice, see the <a class="link" href="rationale-and-impact#information-and-support-2" target="_top" rel="internal" data-chapter="rationale-and-impact" data-original-url="#information-and-support-2">rationale and impact section on information and support</a>.</p>
      <p>Full details of the evidence and the committee's discussion are in <a class="link" href="https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/ng132/evidence/k-patient-information-pdf-244177580630" target="_top">evidence review K: patient information</a>.</p>
    </div>

  <div class="section" title="Terms used in this guideline" id="ng132-terms-used-in-this-guideline">
    <h3 class="title" id="terms-used-in-this-guideline">Terms used in this guideline</h3>
    <div class="section" title="Advice from a specialist" id="ng132-advice-from-a-specialist">
      <h4 class="title" id="advice-from-a-specialist">Advice from a specialist</h4>
      <p>This may be a referral or a telephone call to a specialist.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="section" title="Chronic non-differentiated symptoms" id="ng132-chronic-non-differentiated-symptoms">
      <h4 class="title" id="chronic-non-differentiated-symptoms">Chronic non-differentiated symptoms</h4>
      <p>Long-term symptoms that could have a number of different causes. Some of these symptoms are experienced by people with primary hyperparathyroidism, but they can also be symptoms of other conditions. Examples include fatigue, mild confusion, bone, muscle or joint pain, anxiety, depression, irritability, low mood, apathy, insomnia, frequent urination, increased thirst and digestive problems.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="section" title="Hypercalcaemia" id="ng132-hypercalcaemia">
      <h4 class="title" id="hypercalcaemia">Hypercalcaemia</h4>
      <p>A high level of calcium in the blood, defined as an albumin-adjusted serum calcium level of 2.6 mmol/litre or above. Although hypercalcaemia often causes few or no symptoms, some people feel unusually thirsty, need to urinate frequently or become constipated. </p>
    </div>
