<div class="chapter" title="Recommendations" id="ng77-recommendations" xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml">
  <h2 class="title" id="recommendations">Recommendations</h2>
  <div class="panel panel--primary">
    <p>People have the right to be involved in discussions and make informed decisions about their care, as described in <a class="link" href="https://www.nice.org.uk/about/nice-communities/nice-and-the-public/making-decisions-about-your-care" target="_top">NICE's information on making decisions about your care</a>.</p>
    <p>
      <a class="link" href="https://www.nice.org.uk/about/what-we-do/our-programmes/nice-guidance/nice-guidelines/using-NICE-guidelines-to-make-decisions" target="_top">Making decisions using NICE guidelines</a> explains how we use words to show the strength (or certainty) of our recommendations, and has information about prescribing medicines (including off-label use), professional guidelines, standards and laws (including on consent and mental capacity), and safeguarding.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="section" title="1.1 Patient information" id="ng77-patient-information">
    <h3 class="title" id="patient-information">1.1 Patient information</h3>
    <article id="ng77-1_1_1" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
      <h4 class="recommendation__number">1.1.1</h4>
      <div class="recommendation__body">
        <p>Give people with cataracts, and their family members or carers (as appropriate), both oral and written information. Information should be tailored to the person's needs, for example, in an accessible format. For more guidance on giving information to people and discussing their preferences, see the <a class="link" href="https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/cg138" target="_top" data-original-url="http://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/cg138">NICE guideline on patient experience in adult NHS services</a>, particularly recommendations 1.2.12 and 1.2.13 on capacity and consent. For guidance on eye tests for people living with dementia, see the section on <a class="link" href="https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/ng97/chapter/Recommendations#sensory-impairment" target="_top">sensory impairment in the NICE guideline on dementia</a>.</p>
      </div>
    </article>
    <div class="section" title="At referral for cataract surgery" id="ng77-at-referral-for-cataract-surgery">
      <h4 class="title" id="at-referral-for-cataract-surgery">At referral for cataract surgery</h4>
      <article id="ng77-1_1_2" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.1.2</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>At referral for cataract surgery, give people information about:</p>
          <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>cataracts:</p>
              <p>
                <div class="itemizedlist">
                  <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
                    <li class="listitem">
                      <p>what cataracts are</p>
                    </li>
                    <li class="listitem">
                      <p>how they can affect vision</p>
                    </li>
                    <li class="listitem">
                      <p>how they can affect quality of life</p>
                    </li>
                  </ul>
                </div>
              </p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>cataract surgery:</p>
              <p>
                <div class="itemizedlist">
                  <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
                    <li class="listitem">
                      <p>what it involves and how long it takes</p>
                    </li>
                    <li class="listitem">
                      <p>possible risks and benefits</p>
                    </li>
                    <li class="listitem">
                      <p>what support might be needed after surgery</p>
                    </li>
                    <li class="listitem">
                      <p>likely recovery time</p>
                    </li>
                    <li class="listitem">
                      <p>likely long-term outcomes, including the possibility that people might need spectacles for some tasks</p>
                    </li>
                    <li class="listitem">
                      <p>how vision and quality of life may be affected without surgery.</p>
                    </li>
                  </ul>
                </div>
              </p>
            </li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </article>
    </div>
    <div class="section" title="Before cataract surgery" id="ng77-before-cataract-surgery">
      <h4 class="title" id="before-cataract-surgery">Before cataract surgery</h4>
      <article id="ng77-1_1_3" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.1.3</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>At the preoperative outpatient appointment, review and expand on the topics in recommendation 1.1.2, and give people information about:</p>
          <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>the refractive implications of different intraocular lenses (see recommendation 1.5.3)</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>types of anaesthesia </p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>the person's individual risk of complications during or after surgery (for example, the risk of postoperative retinal detachment in people with high myopia; also see recommendations 1.3.10 and 1.3.11)</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>what to do and what to expect on the day of cataract surgery</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>what to do and what to expect after cataract surgery</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>what support might be needed after surgery</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>medicines after surgery (for example, eye drops) and medicines that people may be already taking (for example, anticoagulants) </p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>the refractive implications after previous corneal refractive surgery, if appropriate (see recommendation 1.3.6)</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>bilateral simultaneous cataract surgery, if appropriate (also see recommendations 1.6.3 and 1.6.4).</p>
            </li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </article>
    </div>
    <div class="section" title="On the day of cataract surgery" id="ng77-on-the-day-of-cataract-surgery">
      <h4 class="title" id="on-the-day-of-cataract-surgery">On the day of cataract surgery</h4>
      <article id="ng77-1_1_4" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.1.4</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>On the day of surgery, before the operation, give people information about:</p>
          <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>their position on the list</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>what to expect during and after surgery.</p>
            </li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng77-1_1_5" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.1.5</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>On the day of surgery, after the operation, give people information about:</p>
          <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>what visual changes to expect</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>signs and symptoms of potential complications to look out for</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>any restrictions on activities, for example, driving</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>possible problems and who to contact</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>emergency situations and who to contact</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>eye drops</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>pain management</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>their next appointment and who they will see.</p>
            </li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </article>
    </div>
    <div class="section" title="After cataract surgery" id="ng77-after-cataract-surgery">
      <h4 class="title" id="after-cataract-surgery">After cataract surgery</h4>
      <article id="ng77-1_1_6" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.1.6</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>At the first appointment after cataract surgery, give people information about:</p>
          <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>eye drops</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>what to do if their vision changes </p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>who to contact if they have concerns or queries </p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>when it is appropriate to get new spectacles and how to do so </p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>second-eye cataract surgery if there is a cataract in the non-operated eye </p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>arrangements for managing ocular comorbidities.</p>
            </li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </article>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="section" title="1.2 Referral for cataract surgery" id="ng77-referral-for-cataract-surgery">
    <h3 class="title" id="referral-for-cataract-surgery">1.2 Referral for cataract surgery</h3>
    <article id="ng77-1_2_1" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
      <h4 class="recommendation__number">1.2.1</h4>
      <div class="recommendation__body">
        <p>Base the decision to refer a person with a cataract for surgery on a discussion with them (and their family members or carers, as appropriate) that includes:</p>
        <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
          <li class="listitem">
            <p>how the cataract affects the person's vision and quality of life</p>
          </li>
          <li class="listitem">
            <p>whether 1 or both eyes are affected </p>
          </li>
          <li class="listitem">
            <p>what cataract surgery involves, including possible risks and benefits</p>
          </li>
          <li class="listitem">
            <p>how the person's quality of life may be affected if they choose not to have cataract surgery</p>
          </li>
          <li class="listitem">
            <p>whether the person wants to have cataract surgery.</p>
          </li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </article>
    <article id="ng77-1_2_2" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
      <h4 class="recommendation__number">1.2.2</h4>
      <div class="recommendation__body">
        <p>Do not restrict access to cataract surgery on the basis of visual acuity.</p>
      </div>
    </article>
  </div>
  <div class="section" title="1.3 Preoperative assessment and biometry" id="ng77-preoperative-assessment-and-biometry">
    <h3 class="title" id="preoperative-assessment-and-biometry">1.3 Preoperative assessment and biometry</h3>
    <div class="section" title="Biometry techniques" id="ng77-biometry-techniques">
      <h4 class="title" id="biometry-techniques">Biometry techniques</h4>
      <article id="ng77-1_3_1" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.3.1</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Use optical biometry to measure the axial length of the eye for people having cataract surgery.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng77-1_3_2" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.3.2</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Use ultrasound biometry if optical biometry:</p>
          <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>is not possible <strong>or</strong></p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>does not give accurate measurements.</p>
            </li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng77-1_3_3" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.3.3</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Use keratometry to measure the curvature of the cornea for people having cataract surgery.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng77-1_3_4" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.3.4</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Consider corneal topography for people having cataract surgery:</p>
          <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>who have abnormally flat or steep corneas</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>who have irregular corneas</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>who have significant astigmatism</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>who have had previous corneal refractive surgery <strong>or</strong></p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>if it is not possible to get an accurate keratometry measurement.</p>
            </li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </article>
    </div>
    <div class="section" title="Biometry formulas" id="ng77-biometry-formulas">
      <h4 class="title" id="biometry-formulas">Biometry formulas</h4>
      <article id="ng77-1_3_5" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.3.5</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>For people who have not had previous corneal refractive surgery, use 1 of the following to calculate the intraocular lens power before cataract surgery:</p>
          <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>If the axial length is less than 22.00 mm, use Haigis or Hoffer Q.</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>If the axial length is between 22.00 and 26.00 mm, use Barrett Universal II if it is installed on the biometry device and does not need the results to be transcribed by hand. Use SRK/T if not.</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>If the axial length is more than 26.00 mm, use Haigis or SRK/T.</p>
            </li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng77-1_3_6" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.3.6</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Advise people who have had previous corneal refractive surgery that refractive outcomes after cataract surgery are difficult to predict, and that they may need further surgery if they do not want to wear spectacles for distance vision.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng77-1_3_7" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.3.7</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>If people have had previous corneal refractive surgery, adjust for the altered relationship between the anterior and posterior corneal curvature. Do not use standard biometry techniques or historical data alone.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng77-1_3_8" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.3.8</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Surgeons should think about modifying a manufacturer's recommended intraocular lens constant, guided by learning gained from their previous deviations from predicted refractive outcomes.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
    </div>
    <div class="section" title="Second-eye prediction" id="ng77-second-eye-prediction">
      <h4 class="title" id="second-eye-prediction">Second-eye prediction</h4>
      <article id="ng77-1_3_9" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.3.9</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Consider using 50% of the first-eye prediction error in observed refractive outcome to guide calculations for the intraocular lens power for second-eye cataract surgery.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
    </div>
    <div class="section" title="Risk stratification" id="ng77-risk-stratification">
      <h4 class="title" id="risk-stratification">Risk stratification</h4>
      <article id="ng77-1_3_10" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.3.10</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Consider using a validated risk stratification algorithm for people who have been referred for cataract surgery, to identify people at increased risk of complications during and after surgery.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng77-1_3_11" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.3.11</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Explain the results of the risk stratification to the person, and discuss how it may affect their decisions.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng77-1_3_12" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.3.12</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>To minimise the risk of complications during and after surgery, ensure that surgeons in training are closely supervised when they perform cataract surgery in: </p>
          <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>people who are at high risk of complications <strong>or</strong></p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>people for whom the impact of complications would be especially severe (for example, people with only 1 functional eye).</p>
            </li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng77-1_3_13" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.3.13</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Explain to people who are at risk of developing a dense cataract that there is an increased risk of complications if surgery is delayed and the cataract becomes more dense.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="section" title="1.4 Intraocular lens selection" id="ng77-intraocular-lens-selection">

      <h4 class="recommendation__number">1.4.4</h4>

    <div class="section" title="Addressing pre-existing astigmatism" id="ng77-addressing-pre-existing-astigmatism">

  <div class="section" title="1.5 Preventing wrong lens implant errors" id="ng77-preventing-wrong-lens-implant-errors">
    <h3 class="title" id="preventing-wrong-lens-implant-errors">1.5 Preventing wrong lens implant errors</h3>
    <div class="section" title="Before cataract surgery" id="ng77-before-cataract-surgery-2">
      <h4 class="title" id="before-cataract-surgery-2">Before cataract surgery</h4>
      <article id="ng77-1_5_1" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.5.1</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Before the preoperative biometry assessment, ensure that the person's correct medical notes are used by confirming the person's:</p>
          <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>name</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>address <strong>and</strong></p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>date of birth.</p>
            </li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng77-1_5_2" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.5.2</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Immediately after the preoperative biometry assessment:</p>
          <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>check that the biometry results include the person's name, address, date of birth and hospital number</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>either</p>
              <p>
                <div class="itemizedlist">
                  <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
                    <li class="listitem">
                      <p>use electronic data transfer to upload the biometry results to an electronic health record <strong>or</strong></p>
                    </li>
                    <li class="listitem">
                      <p>securely fix the printed biometry results to the person's medical notes</p>
                    </li>
                  </ul>
                </div>
              </p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>do not transcribe the results by hand.</p>
            </li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng77-1_5_3" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.5.3</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>At the preoperative assessment:</p>
          <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>discuss the refractive implications of different intraocular lenses with the person</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>base the choice of intraocular lens on the person's chosen refractive outcome </p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>record the discussion and the person's choices in their medical notes.</p>
            </li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </article>
    </div>
    <div class="section" title="On the day of cataract surgery" id="ng77-on-the-day-of-cataract-surgery-2">
      <h4 class="title" id="on-the-day-of-cataract-surgery-2">On the day of cataract surgery</h4>
      <article id="ng77-1_5_4" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.5.4</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>The person's medical notes, including biometry results, must be available in theatre on the day of the cataract surgery.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng77-1_5_5" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.5.5</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Use a checklist based on the <a class="link" href="http://www.who.int/patientsafety/safesurgery/checklist/en/" target="_top">World Health Organization (WHO) surgical safety checklist</a>, modified to include the following cataract surgery checks, to ensure that:</p>
          <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>the person's identity has been confirmed and matches information in:</p>
              <p>
                <div class="itemizedlist">
                  <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
                    <li class="listitem">
                      <p>the consent form</p>
                    </li>
                    <li class="listitem">
                      <p>the biometry results <strong>and</strong></p>
                    </li>
                    <li class="listitem">
                      <p>the person's medical notes</p>
                    </li>
                  </ul>
                </div>
              </p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>the eye to be operated on has been checked and clearly marked</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>there is only 1 intraocular lens in the theatre, that matches the person's selected lens type and prescription</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>at least 1 additional identical intraocular lens is in stock</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>alternative intraocular lenses are in stock in case the selected lens needs to be changed if there are complications during surgery</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>at least 2 members of the team, including the surgeon, have previously checked the appropriateness, accuracy and consistency of all:</p>
              <p>
                <div class="itemizedlist">
                  <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
                    <li class="listitem">
                      <p>formulas</p>
                    </li>
                    <li class="listitem">
                      <p>calculations <strong>and</strong></p>
                    </li>
                    <li class="listitem">
                      <p>intraocular lens constants.</p>
                    </li>
                  </ul>
                </div>
              </p>
            </li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng77-1_5_6" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.5.6</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Before giving the person anaesthetic, ensure that: </p>
          <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>there is only 1 intraocular lens in the theatre, that matches the person's selected lens type and prescription</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>at least 1 additional identical intraocular lens is in stock</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>alternative intraocular lenses are in stock in case the selected lens needs to be changed if there are complications during surgery.</p>
            </li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng77-1_5_7" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.5.7</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Immediately before the operation, the surgeon should:</p>
          <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>confirm the person's identity and ensure that the correct medical notes are being used, especially if using electronic patient records</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>refer to the printed biometry results, not to transcribed information in the person's medical notes</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>refer to the person's medical notes to check which refractive outcome they preferred</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>verify that the correct intraocular lens has been selected and is available in theatre.</p>
            </li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </article>
    </div>
    <div class="section" title="Occurrence of wrong lens implant errors" id="ng77-occurrence-of-wrong-lens-implant-errors">
      <h4 class="title" id="occurrence-of-wrong-lens-implant-errors">Occurrence of wrong lens implant errors</h4>
      <article id="ng77-1_5_8" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.5.8</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>If a wrong lens is implanted, refer to <a class="link" href="http://www.england.nhs.uk/patientsafety/never-events/" target="_top">NHS England's Never Events policy</a>, and together with the whole multidisciplinary team:</p>
          <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>undertake a root-cause analysis to determine the reasons for the incident</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>establish strategies and implementation tools to stop it from happening again.</p>
            </li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </article>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="section" title="1.6 Surgical timing and technique" id="ng77-surgical-timing-and-technique">
    <h3 class="title" id="surgical-timing-and-technique">1.6 Surgical timing and technique</h3>
    <div class="section" title="Laser-assisted cataract surgery" id="ng77-laser-assisted-cataract-surgery">
      <h4 class="title" id="laser-assisted-cataract-surgery">Laser-assisted cataract surgery</h4>
      <article id="ng77-1_6_1" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.6.1</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Only use femtosecond laser-assisted cataract surgery as part of a randomised controlled trial that includes collection of resource-use data, comparing femtosecond laser-assisted cataract surgery with ultrasound phacoemulsification.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
    </div>
    <div class="section" title="Bilateral surgery" id="ng77-bilateral-surgery">
      <h4 class="title" id="bilateral-surgery">Bilateral surgery</h4>
      <article id="ng77-1_6_2" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.6.2</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Offer second-eye cataract surgery using the same criteria as for the first-eye surgery (see <a class="link" href="recommendations#referral-for-cataract-surgery" target="_top" rel="internal" data-chapter="recommendations" data-original-url="#referral-for-cataract-surgery">referral for cataract surgery</a>).</p>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng77-1_6_3" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.6.3</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Consider bilateral simultaneous cataract surgery for:</p>
          <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>people who are at low risk of ocular complications during and after surgery <strong>or</strong></p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>people who need to have general anaesthesia for cataract surgery but for whom general anaesthesia carries an increased risk of complications or distress.</p>
            </li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng77-1_6_4" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.6.4</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Discuss the potential risks and benefits of bilateral simultaneous cataract surgery with people, which should include:</p>
          <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>the potential immediate visual improvement in both eyes</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>how it will not be possible to choose a different intraocular lens based on the outcome in the first eye</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>the risk of complications in both eyes during and after surgery that could cause long-term visual impairment</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>the likely need for additional support after the operation.</p>
            </li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </article>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="section" title="1.7 Anaesthesia" id="ng77-anaesthesia">
    <h3 class="title" id="anaesthesia">1.7 Anaesthesia</h3>
    <article id="ng77-1_7_1" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
      <h4 class="recommendation__number">1.7.1</h4>
      <div class="recommendation__body">
        <p>Offer sub‑Tenon's or topical (with or without intracameral) anaesthesia for people having cataract surgery.</p>
      </div>
    </article>
    <article id="ng77-1_7_2" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
      <h4 class="recommendation__number">1.7.2</h4>
      <div class="recommendation__body">
        <p>If both sub‑Tenon's and topical (with or without intracameral) anaesthesia are contraindicated, consider peribulbar anaesthesia.</p>
      </div>
    </article>
    <article id="ng77-1_7_3" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
      <h4 class="recommendation__number">1.7.3</h4>
      <div class="recommendation__body">
        <p>Do not offer retrobulbar anaesthesia for people having cataract surgery.</p>
      </div>
    </article>
    <article id="ng77-1_7_4" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
      <h4 class="recommendation__number">1.7.4</h4>
      <div class="recommendation__body">
        <p>Consider sedation, administered by an experienced ophthalmic anaesthetist, as an adjunct to anaesthesia for people if, for example:</p>
        <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
          <li class="listitem">
            <p>they have high levels of anxiety</p>
          </li>
          <li class="listitem">
            <p>they have postural or musculoskeletal problems</p>
          </li>
          <li class="listitem">
            <p>surgery is expected to take longer than usual.</p>
          </li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </article>
    <article id="ng77-1_7_5" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
      <h4 class="recommendation__number">1.7.5</h4>
      <div class="recommendation__body">
        <p>Consider hyaluronidase as an adjunct to sub‑Tenon's anaesthesia, particularly if trying to stop the eye moving during surgery.</p>
      </div>
    </article>
  </div>
  <div class="section" title="1.8 Preventing and managing complications" id="ng77-preventing-and-managing-complications">
    <h3 class="title" id="preventing-and-managing-complications">1.8 Preventing and managing complications</h3>
    <div class="section" title="Floppy iris syndrome" id="ng77-floppy-iris-syndrome">
      <h4 class="title" id="floppy-iris-syndrome">Floppy iris syndrome</h4>
      <article id="ng77-1_8_1" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.8.1</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Consider intracameral phenylephrine to increase pupil size in people at risk of floppy iris syndrome.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
    </div>
    <div class="section" title="Capsular tension rings" id="ng77-capsular-tension-rings">
      <h4 class="title" id="capsular-tension-rings">Capsular tension rings</h4>
      <article id="ng77-1_8_2" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.8.2</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Do not use capsular tension rings in routine, uncomplicated cataract surgery.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng77-1_8_3" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.8.3</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Consider using capsular tension rings for people with pseudoexfoliation.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
    </div>
    <div class="section" title="Endophthalmitis" id="ng77-endophthalmitis">
      <h4 class="title" id="endophthalmitis">Endophthalmitis</h4>
      <article id="ng77-1_8_4" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.8.4</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Use preoperative antiseptics in line with standard surgical practice.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng77-1_8_5" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.8.5</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Use intracameral cefuroxime during cataract surgery to prevent endophthalmitis.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng77-1_8_6" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.8.6</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Use commercially prepared or pharmacy-prepared intracameral antibiotic solutions to prevent dilution errors.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
    </div>
    <div class="section" title="Cystoid macular oedema" id="ng77-cystoid-macular-oedema">
      <h4 class="title" id="cystoid-macular-oedema">Cystoid macular oedema</h4>
      <article id="ng77-1_8_7" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.8.7</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Consider topical steroids in combination with non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs):</p>
          <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>after cataract surgery for people at increased risk of cystoid macular oedema, for example, people with diabetes or uveitis</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>to manage cystoid macular oedema.</p>
            </li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </article>
      <article id="ng77-1_8_8" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.8.8</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Offer topical steroids and/or NSAIDs after cataract surgery to prevent inflammation and cystoid macular oedema.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
    </div>
    <div class="section" title="Posterior capsule rupture" id="ng77-posterior-capsule-rupture">
      <h4 class="title" id="posterior-capsule-rupture">Posterior capsule rupture</h4>
      <article id="ng77-1_8_9" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.8.9</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>When dealing with posterior capsule rupture, follow a protocol that covers:</p>
          <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>removing vitreous from the wound and anterior chamber</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>minimising traction on the retina</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>removing lens fragments in the posterior chamber or vitreous cavity</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>removing soft lens matter</p>
            </li>
            <li class="listitem">
              <p>implications for any lens insertion.</p>
            </li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </article>
    </div>
    <div class="section" title="Postoperative eye protection" id="ng77-postoperative-eye-protection">
      <h4 class="title" id="postoperative-eye-protection">Postoperative eye protection</h4>
      <article id="ng77-1_8_10" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
        <h5 class="recommendation__number">1.8.10</h5>
        <div class="recommendation__body">
          <p>Offer eye protection for people whose eye shows residual effects of anaesthesia at the time of discharge after cataract surgery.</p>
        </div>
      </article>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="section" title="1.9 Postoperative assessment" id="ng77-postoperative-assessment">
    <h3 class="title" id="postoperative-assessment">1.9 Postoperative assessment</h3>
    <article id="ng77-1_9_1" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
      <h4 class="recommendation__number">1.9.1</h4>
      <div class="recommendation__body">
        <p>Commissioners and service providers should ensure that the following are in place:</p>
        <ul class="itemizedlist indented">
          <li class="listitem">
            <p>Processes that identify complications after surgery and ensure that there is prompt access to specialist ophthalmology services.</p>
          </li>
          <li class="listitem">
            <p>Processes to ensure that the <a class="link" href="https://www.rcophth.ac.uk/standards-publications-research/national-ophthalmology-database/" target="_top">UK Minimum Cataract Dataset for National Audit</a> is completed.</p>
          </li>
          <li class="listitem">
            <p>Arrangements so that healthcare professionals discuss second-eye cataract surgery with people who have a cataract in their non-operated eye.</p>
          </li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </article>
    <article id="ng77-1_9_2" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
      <h4 class="recommendation__number">1.9.2</h4>
      <div class="recommendation__body">
        <p>Consider collecting patient visual function and quality-of-life data for entry into an electronic dataset.</p>
      </div>
    </article>
    <article id="ng77-1_9_3" class="numbered-paragraph recommendation">
      <h4 class="recommendation__number">1.9.3</h4>
      <div class="recommendation__body">
        <p>Do not offer in‑person, first-day review to people after uncomplicated cataract surgery.</p>
      </div>
    </article>
  </div>
