# Concept C — Compact doctor workstation

Generated mockup: `concept_c_compact_workstation.png`

## Direction

- Dense, fast workstation with a persistent sidebar and split input/output surfaces.
- Quick Note and Detailed Note are optimized for a 13-inch clinic laptop.
- Workflows, chips, and note output stay visible together.

## Strengths

- Highest information density and fastest repeated-use potential.
- Strong persistent draft visibility.
- Compact sections are efficient for experienced users.

## Risks

- Feels closer to an EHR shell than a focused documentation tool.
- Sidebar concepts such as patients, favorites, and templates imply unavailable product features.
- More difficult to simplify on mobile.
- Highest risk of feeling clunky for first-time testers.
