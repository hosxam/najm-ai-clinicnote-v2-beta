# Concept B — Premium medical productivity workspace

Generated mockup: `concept_b_premium_productivity.png`

## Direction

- Light neutral workspace with white elevated surfaces and a restrained teal-blue accent.
- Workflow search remains central, with a compact product shell instead of a marketing page.
- Quick Note uses a two-column input and draft-review layout.
- Suggested defaults read as intentional, reviewable selections.
- Detailed Note retains structure without visually competing with Quick Note.

## Strengths

- Best balance of clarity, polish, and clinical review visibility.
- Makes Quick Note feel like the main product workflow.
- The sticky draft surface gives copy, print, and review actions a clear home.
- Adapts well to a single-column mobile layout.

## Risks

- Requires disciplined spacing to avoid returning to card stacking.
- The light workspace is a meaningful departure from the current dark presentation.
- Dense workflows still need careful chip wrapping and vertical rhythm.
